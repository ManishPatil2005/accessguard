📋 Quick Steps for Render

  1. Go to render.com
  2. Click Sign Up
  3. Click New Web Service
  4. Paste repository: https://github.com/ManishPatil2005/accessguard
  5. Click Deploy
  6. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
  7. Get your live URL (copy and share!)
Your live app URL will look like: https://accessguard-abc123.onrender.com
⚠️ Note: After deployment, first page load may take 30 seconds. This is normal - the app is starting up. It will be faster after that.

🧪 Test Your Deployment

Once live, test these features:

  1. Register: Create new account with email and password
  2. Login: Use your credentials to access dashboard
  3. Test Security: Try wrong password 3 times (account locks)
  4. Admin Panel: View audit logs and locked accounts
